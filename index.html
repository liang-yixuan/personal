About Me

Yixuan is a graduate student studying Information Technology at the University of Melbourne.

She graduated from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen in June 2019 and worked as data scientist intern at both Tencent, one of largest Internet-based platform company in the world, and China Guangfa Bank, a commercial banking corporation.

Outside of professional life, Yixuan enjoys traveling and embraces all the cultural differences she can get from her trips.

During her undergraduate study, she studied at 4 universities in 4 continents including Asia, North America, Europe, and Oceania. With the opportunities to study abroad, she traveled many places around the world and learned a lot outside of school.
